About Us

Bokamoso Property Consultancy (Pty) LTD is a leading real estate and property management company that specializes in house planning, construction, property surveys, real estate investments, and legal services related to property transactions.

Established in 2020, we have built a reputation for providing high-quality architectural and engineering solutions, as well as offering expert guidance in property evaluations, land surveys, and legal consultations for property ownership and transactions.

We take pride in helping our clients find, develop, and manage properties with professionalism and integrity. As a fully registered and compliant company, we ensure that all our services adhere to legal standards and regulations, providing our clients with peace of mind in every transaction.
